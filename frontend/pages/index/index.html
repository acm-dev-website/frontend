About UCF ACM

University of Central Florida`s Association for Computing Machinery chapter is one of the largest pre-professional computer science organizations. Focusing on bridging the gap between computer science, computer engineering, and other disciples of of engineering, we aim to have members seek new passions, develop new interests, meet new people and build lifelong friendships, and be ready for the professional workforce.

What We Do

Through general events, projects specific to certain disciples, workshops oriented to learning new skills, socials dedicated to finding a community, we aim to develop the technical and professional skills students develop to be ready for the next stage in their lives. Explore the site to find out what we have planned!

Upcoming Workshop

Machine Learning Workshop

The Introduction to Machine Learning Workshop is designed to provide participants with a comprehensive understanding of machine learning concepts, algorithms, and practical applications. Whether you're a beginner or have some experience in data science, this workshop will equip you with the knowledge and skills needed to start building and deploying machine learning models.
